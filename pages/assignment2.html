Assignment 2 - Embedded Carto Timeseries

Carto is a web-based platform aimed at simplifying the process of designing and publishing maps with varying levels of analytical complexity, ranging from simple choropleth and time-series maps to complex locational intelligence processing. Assignment 2 for GEOG774 requires the student to produce an animated time-series map of road collision events in Auckland, using data obtained from the New Zealand Transport Agency (NZTA) through the Auckland hackathon event 'HACKAKL'. The output of this assignment is below: an animated time-series map created in Carto Builder and embedded in this Github-hosted webpage.
